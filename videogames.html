I love videogames! From Single Player RPG to Multiplayer Tactical Shooter, I always come back for more. Here's a short list of games I've played recently and my reviews for them!
(Also check out my playtime!)

Metaphor:
ReFantazio

Playtime: 115 Hours

A thrilling expierence from start to finish, Metaphor:ReFantazio surely will not dissapoint any to touch it. If you want a beautifully witten story with challenging and strategic battles, Metaphor is for you!

Persona 3 Reload

Playtime: 100 Hours

What is the meaning of Life? What is the meaning of Death? Persona 3 asks these questions with beautiful poise and storytelling. With a combat system with enough style to make any artist impressed, Persona 3 Reload gives an expierence that not many can forget.

Valorant

Playtime: 525 Hours

Valorant is the main multiplayer game me and my friends play. It's really frustrating, but also really fun a lot of times with the right people! If you like competitive shooters, try Valorant!

South Park:
The Stick of Truth

Playtime: 11 Hours

Add dark comedy and a simple but fun rpg combat system, and what do you get? South Park but Dungeons and Dragons! Need a short game that will have you rolling with immature and crude humor? Then check out South Park: The Stick of Truth!

Kingdom Hearts
Birth by Sleep: Final Mix

Playtime: 35 Hours

I've been expierencing the Kingdom Hearts series from start to finish, and Birth by Sleep fits like a glove in the Kingdom Hearts franchise. Short, sweet, and full of extras; Birth by Sleep hits all the right notes. (P.S. Don't start the series with this game, go in release order.)

BioShock

Playtime: 10 Hours

What if Humanity could alter their genetic structure with just the tip of a needle? Venture to the bottom of the ocean to save a city that collapsed upon itself with it's own greed. Use guns and your own reworked body to fend off humans that messed with their gene pool to the point of no return. Just remember, in Rapture, there are no gods or kings, only man.

Pokémon:
Soul Silver

Playtime: 46 Hours

Hilariously hard and painstakingly pleasing, Soul Silver takes the gloves off and makes you earn your wins. Wanna get your ass beat in a retro vibe with cute animal creatures? Play Soul Silver!

Fire Emblem Awakening

Playtime: 55 Hours

My first ever real Fire Emblem expierence, Awakening allowed me to fully submerse myself into the Fire Emblem universe. If you think chess isn't hard enough and want to add some magic to it, then play Fire Emblem awakening!
